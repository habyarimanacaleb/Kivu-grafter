SIX ISLANDS KIVU TOURS

Taking a guided boat tour on Lake Kivu offers a fascinating exploration of its islands, each with its own distinctive charm and history. Discover the perfect blend of cultural heritage, natural beauty, and adventure.

Boat Tour Overview

Your journey begins aboard a comfortable boat equipped for exploration and relaxation. Enjoy the scenic beauty of Lake Kivu’s clear waters and volcanic backdrop.

Exploration of Islands

Visit three unique islands, each offering a glimpse into Lake Kivu’s cultural and natural heritage.

Punishment Island

Historical Significance

Punishment Island, or ‘Akanyaru,’ reflects a somber history where women accused of adultery were exiled. Learn about its cultural significance during guided explorations.

Guided Exploration

Guides provide historical insights and share stories about the island’s role in local traditions.

Farm Island

Agricultural Delight

Stroll through fields of bananas and coffee plants while learning about sustainable farming practices.

Culinary Experiences

Sample fresh produce and beverages made from the island’s fertile lands.

Peace Island

Scenic Beauty

Relax with panoramic views of Lake Kivu and hike its tranquil trails.

Adventurous Activities

Enjoy thrilling activities like tree jumping into the lake’s waters.

Guided Experience

Knowledgeable guides enrich your journey with stories of the island’s ecology and history.

Comfort and Amenities

The tour ensures comfort with shaded seating, ample space, and relaxing onboard amenities.

Cultural Exchange

Interact with local guides and communities to gain deeper insights into Rwandan traditions.
